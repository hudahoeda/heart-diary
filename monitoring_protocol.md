## ðŸ“‹ Pre-Recording Checklist
1.  **Moisture:** Ensure the Polar H10 strap electrodes are **wet**. (Water or electrode gel is best). Dry contacts cause noise that looks like PVCs.

4.  **Posture:**
    *   Sit up straight or lie flat.
    *   **Do not slouch** (compressing the chest can create artifacts).
    *   Rest your hands on your lap (don't use your phone/type during the baseline recording).
